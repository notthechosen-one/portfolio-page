Car

A car is an automobile that has four wheels and seats for small number of people. There are different types of car: Sedan, Hatchback, Coupe, SUV, Sportscar, Convertible, Crossover, Muscle Car, Station Wagon, Pickup Truck, Jeep, and Limousine. These cars are used to traverse roads easier and help people travel from far distances.


Example of each type of car:
Sedan:

Honda Civic, Toyota Vios, Volkswagen Virtus, Mitsubishi Mirage G4

Hatchback:

Toyota Wigo, BYD Dolphin, Mazda 3 Fastback

SUV:

Nissan Terra, Toyota Fortuner, Honda CR-V
 Coupe:
 Porsche 911, Ford Mustang, Audi R8
Sportscar:
 Toyota Supra, Mazda Miata, BMW M2
